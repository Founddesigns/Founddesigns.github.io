Section 1

What are cookies and other comparable technologies?

Cookies are text files that are downloaded to your device when you visit a website. They are useful because they allow a website to recognize a user's device. You can find more information about cookies here: www.allaboutcookies.org or www.youronlinechoices.eu.

‍

The term "cookie" actually encompassesvarious technologies, including: Pixel tags (transparent graphic images that are placed on a website or in an email and indicate that a page or email has been viewed), Device identifier and Web storage used in desktop software or mobile devices.

We use the term "cookie" in this policy to refer to all of these technologies. We make sure to provide you with clear information about all technologies so that you can make informed decisions about your cookie settings.

Cookies do many different things. For example,they allow you to navigate efficiently between different pages, remember your settings and generally provide a better user experience. They can also help make the ads you see online more relevant to you and your interests.

‍

There are different categories of cookies,including:

Session and persistent cookies

Session cookies - these cookies expire as soon as you close the web browser.

Persistent cookies - these cookies remain on your device for a set period of time or until you delete them.

‍

First and third party cookies

First-party cookies - these cookies come from the website you are currently visiting, either from us or from a third party.

Third party cookies - these cookies come from a third party who is independent of the website you are visiting.

The Information We Collect:

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the Ubiquity Developments website. On some pages you can order services and make requests. The types of personal information collected at these pages are: Name, Address, Email Address, Phone Number.

The Way We Use Information:

We  use the information you provide about yourself when placing an order only to complete that order. If a user elects to use our referral service for informing a friend about our site, we ask them for the friend's name and email address. This privacy statement applies soley to information collected by this website.

Third Party Advertising:

We do not sell, distribute or lease your personal information to third parties unless we are given permission to do so or are required to do so by law.

Correspondence:

Ubiquity Developments may send you service-related announcements from time to time through e-mail.
